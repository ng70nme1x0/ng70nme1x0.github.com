Frequently Asked Questions

  • How is this different than the DAO and other investment initiatives?

      How is this different than the DAO and other investment initiatives?

      The DAO was an ambitious initiative to launch a democratic VC firm where the votes of participants determine which initiative is funded by how much. The Unit Fund is fundamentally different -- we simply enable individuals to invest into the VC fund; the fund is managed by professionals and not by “the crowd”.

  • What is a UFT?

      What is a UFT?

      A UFT, or Unit Fund Token, represents a Proof-of-Membership in a fund. It grants access to fund profits, executed by an Ethereum smart contract.

  • Is this an existing fund?

      Is this an existing fund?

      No, Unit Fund will be a new fund. Upon completion of the token sale, we will establish the closed-ended fund in the respective jurisdictions.

  • How do I receive my investment dues?

      How do I receive my investment dues?

      Returns are delivered in the form of ETH directly to your originating token address. For this reason, we require that you use a wallet and NOT send funds directly from an exchange.

  • How are investment opportunities selected? Do I have to vote on them?

      How are investment opportunities selected? Do I have to vote on them?

      Investment opportunities are sourced through our partnerships with world-class international accelerators like YCombinator and 500 Startups. We do not require investors to get involved in the selection process, as our management team vets and reviews every opportunity before making a decision to invest.

  • What safeguards do you employ to protect my investment?

      What safeguards do you use to protect my investment?

      It's important to verify that the funds you invest are protected. We've setup a smart contract at an address that will be revealed just before the Tokensale start date. We've also included the source code so you can review it.

  • What happens if you don't raise the $1,000,000 minimum?

      What happens if you don't raise the $1,000,000 minimum?

      If we're unable to raise the minimum fund amount, we'll return all funds to their investors. This is done programmatically, through the smart contract.

  • Are there any restrictions on who can invest?

      Are there any restrictions on who can invest?

      You are only allowed to purchase Unit Fund Tokens (UFTs) if you are neither a U.S. citizen or permanent resident of the United States, nor have a primary residence or domicile in the United States, including Puerto Rico, the U.S. Virgin Islands, and any other territories of the United States.

  • How long will my investment be tied up in this fund?

      How long will my investment be tied up in this fund?

      This varies, depending on when an individual investment "exits" (either via an acquisition, or an IPO) since we disburse returns as they happen. We typically expect returns within the 5 - 7 year range.

  • Do you guarantee the 20%+ returns on my investment?

      Do you guarantee the 20%+ annual return on my investment?

      No, we do not. Nor does any other VC fund. That said, we do take deal due diligence seriously and hold all potential deals to very high investment standards.
